About the Actors Guild of Nigeria

the heartbeat of Nollywood!

Who We Are

Established over 25 years ago, AGN stands proud as the umbrella body for professional actors in Nigeria's thriving motion picture industry. As the "Face of Nollywood," we are more than just an association - we are a family of talented individuals dedicated to showcasing the very best of Nigerian creativity and culture to the world.

With over two million members spread across 34 states, we are the largest cultural association in Africa. Our reach extends beyond borders as a proud member of the prestigious International Federation of Actors (FIA), allowing us to collaborate with associations and unions worldwide to elevate our industry.

From the early 20th century to today, Nollywood has been on an incredible journey of self-discovery and actualization. Our actors' faces grace screens in bustling city centers and remote villages alike, touching hearts and minds across the globe.

Our Mission

To champion Nigerian culture and tourism through compelling storytelling

To serve as brand ambassadors, projecting a positive image of Nigeria globally

Our Vision

To nurture and support the growth of acting talent across the nation

Our History

The Actors Guild of Nigeria was founded in 1999 as a collective effort by professionals in the entertainment industry. Since its inception, AGN has become a pillar of the Nigerian film industry, advocating for the rights and well-being of actors and establishing itself as a major player in the African film scene.

Through the years, the Guild has implemented several initiatives aimed at fostering the growth of the Nigerian film industry. From capacity-building workshops to international collaborations, AGN remains committed to enhancing the professionalism and welfare of its members.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions and decisions, ensuring trust and transparency in our dealings.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality in everything we do, ensuring that our members excel both locally and internationally.

Unity

We believe in the power of collaboration and work together as a unified body to achieve our goals and elevate the industry.
