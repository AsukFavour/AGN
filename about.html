About the Actors Guild of Nigeria

Uniting the voices of Nigerian actors and shaping the future of the industry.

Who We Are

The Actors Guild of Nigeria (AGN) is the umbrella body representing all professional actors in the Nigerian film and television industry. Established to promote and protect the rights of actors, AGN serves as the voice of the acting profession and works tirelessly to enhance the quality and standards of the Nigerian film industry.

We aim to foster a professional environment that empowers our members through education, advocacy, and the promotion of best practices in the industry.

Our Mission

To uphold the dignity of Nigerian actors and provide a supportive platform for the growth of acting talents while safeguarding the interests and welfare of all our members.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in the global film industry by promoting professionalism, unity, and the global recognition of Nigerian actors.

Our History

The Actors Guild of Nigeria was founded in 1999 as a collective effort by professionals in the entertainment industry. Since its inception, AGN has become a pillar of the Nigerian film industry, advocating for the rights and well-being of actors and establishing itself as a major player in the African film scene.

Through the years, the Guild has implemented several initiatives aimed at fostering the growth of the Nigerian film industry. From capacity-building workshops to international collaborations, AGN remains committed to enhancing the professionalism and welfare of its members.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions and decisions, ensuring trust and transparency in our dealings.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality in everything we do, ensuring that our members excel both locally and internationally.

Unity

We believe in the power of collaboration and work together as a unified body to achieve our goals and elevate the industry.
