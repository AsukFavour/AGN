About the Actors Guild of Nigeria

The Actors Guild of Nigeria (AGN) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare, promotion, and professional development of actors across the country. Our mission is to support, protect, and advocate for the rights of actors while empowering them with the skills and resources needed for a successful career in the entertainment industry.
